# Rating

The `<Rating>` component is used to display a rating as a star-based system.

<Preview withToolbar>
  <Story name="intro">
    <Flex column>
      <Rating rating={1} />
      <Rating rating={1.8} />
      <Rating rating={2} />
      <Rating rating={2.15} />
      <Rating rating={3} />
      <Rating rating={3.5} />
      <Rating rating={4} />
      <Rating rating={4.32} />
      <Rating rating={5} />
    </Flex>
  </Story>
</Preview>

Ratings can be display with their rating count.

<Preview withToolbar>
  <Story name="withRating">
    <Flex column>
      <Rating rating={1} withRating />
      <Rating rating={1.8} withRating />
      <Rating rating={2} withRating />
      <Rating rating={2.15} withRating />
      <Rating rating={3} withRating />
      <Rating rating={3.5} withRating />
      <Rating rating={4} withRating />
      <Rating rating={4.32} withRating />
      <Rating rating={5} withRating />
    </Flex>
  </Story>
</Preview>

Ratings can be display with their total reviews count.

<Preview withToolbar>
  <Story name="withTotal">
    <Flex column>
      <Rating rating={1} total={1} withTotal />
      <Rating rating={1.8} total={23} withTotal />
      <Rating rating={2} total={325} withTotal />
      <Rating rating={2.15} total={9999} withTotal />
      <Rating rating={3} total={6} withTotal />
      <Rating rating={3.5} total={29} withTotal />
      <Rating rating={4} total={311} withTotal />
      <Rating rating={4.32} total={87} withTotal />
      <Rating rating={5} total={1002} withTotal />
    </Flex>
  </Story>
</Preview>

They can also be displayed with both.

<Preview withToolbar>
  <Story name="withTotal + withRating">
    <Flex column>
      <Rating rating={1} total={1} withTotal withRating />
      <Rating rating={1.8} total={23} withTotal withRating />
      <Rating rating={2} total={325} withTotal withRating />
      <Rating rating={2.15} total={9999} withTotal withRating />
      <Rating rating={3} total={6} withTotal withRating />
      <Rating rating={3.5} total={29} withTotal withRating />
      <Rating rating={4} total={311} withTotal withRating />
      <Rating rating={4.32} total={87} withTotal withRating />
      <Rating rating={5} total={1002} withTotal withRating />
    </Flex>
  </Story>
</Preview>

The icon's size can be adjusted via the `scale` prop, which uses the `icons` global token.

<Preview withToolbar>
  <Story name="scale">

      <Rating scale={1} rating={4.32} total={87} withTotal withRating />
      <Rating scale={2} rating={5} total={1002} withTotal withRating />
    </Flex>
  </Story>
</Preview>
